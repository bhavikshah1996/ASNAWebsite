Auti And Sons Natural Agro offers a fair-priced fixed contract rate to the farmers. Because of ongoing value addition to the farmers, there is no default by the farmers in supply. AUTI AND SONS NATURAL AGRO is a most trusted brand amongst farmers in Maharashtra,INDIA Regular training programs are organised for farmers periodically.
