Our Vision

We live in a world where resources are limited. Midway through this century, the global population is predicted to exceed 9 billion people. We therefore face the great challenge of dealing with the need for healthy food together with an ever-increasing shortage of natural resources.

This requires us to evolve and combine the best farming traditions with the most innovative technology. Whilst caring for the Environment, Empowering our stakeholders and strengthening the economy.

Mission

Auti & Son’s Natural Agro goal is very clear: grow first class products that improve people’s health and wellbeing. We offer our clients an efficient service that facilitates the sustainable marketing of fresh produce, whilst striving for cooperation between consumers and suppliers. This is why we supply highly nutritional, fresh products that improve the health of our customers and their families, and which are enjoyed thanks to their delicious flavors combined with their natural fragrance and colour, fresh from the field.

Our processes and tracking system enable us to deliver our fresh produce to metropolitan areas in the best condition possible, where 75% of the population currently lives. Developing a robust supply chain which promises, traceable, safe, healthy affordable-farm produce and value added product from farm to plate. Building respectable relationship with the environment, employees and all other stakeholders

Chairman Speak
Mr. Rekhunana Auti
Chairman Of Auti And Son’s Natural Agro

The seeding of Auti And Sons’s Natural Agro happened in Ahmednagar, India in 2016 with simple intention of ensuring a fair return to the farmers and with the quality produces reaching end users. The idea behind the entire movement was to buy all the fruits and vegetable produce offered by them under the food safety standards. The primary objective was to provide the best quality infrastructure ,production and processing facility to the farmer.

The movement was developed first to its kind branded traceable product mix that would promote sustained growth and that we could cater with the best to the Indian consumer too, by offerening them quality products at fair prices and to do so by achieving economies of scale and costs.

A structure owened by farmer group be the farmers “Best Friends ” and the intention was that the hightest levels of consumers confidence would be reposed in the brand call “ASNA” by Auti And Sons Natural Agro
